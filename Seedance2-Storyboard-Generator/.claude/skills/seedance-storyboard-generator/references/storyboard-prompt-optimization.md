# Seedance 2.0 Prompt Optimization Guide

## Universal Formula

Every effective Seedance 2.0 prompt follows this structure:

**Subject + Action + Scene + Lighting + Camera Language + Style + Image Quality + Constraints**

Every layer matters—especially **Constraints**, which prevent distortion and breakage.

### Formula Example

**Woman walking on the beach**
- 【Subject】A 25-year-old Asian woman, long black hair, wearing a beige knit sweater
- 【Action】Walking slowly on the beach, breeze in hair, natural smile
- 【Scene】Coastal beach at dusk, sunset reflected on the water
- 【Lighting】Warm golden sunset from the side
- 【Camera】Medium follow shot, slow push-in
- 【Style】35mm film texture, cinematic color grade
- 【Quality】4K ultra HD, sharp detail
- 【Constraints】Normal human anatomy, clear face without distortion, smooth natural motion

## Keyword Quick Reference

### Action Description
slow, gentle, continuous, natural, smooth, not stiff, lightly raise hand, slightly lower head, sway in wind, light footsteps

### Camera Language
slow push-in, slight pull-back, steady truck, half-orbit, locked-off shot, stabilized handheld, high angle, low angle, follow shot

### Shot Size
extreme wide, wide, medium, close-up, extreme close-up, macro close-up

### Lighting & Atmosphere
warm yellow light, soft morning glow, backlit silhouette, low saturation, neon reflection, top light, side light

### Style
cinematic, cyberpunk, ink wash, vintage film, dreamy soft glow, anime look, 35mm film texture

### Quality Baseline
4K ultra HD, sharp detail, clear edges, no blur, no ghosting, no flicker

### Constraint Anti-Break
normal human structure, clear face without distortion, stable facial features, natural proportions, motion not stiff, stable frame without shake

## Notes

- **Describe action as slow**—slower is steadier. Avoid words like "exaggerated," "high-speed," or "violent twisting," which often break the image.
- **Avoid subjective words** like "beautiful," "handsome," or "cool"—the model cannot interpret abstract praise; it is as good as omitted.
