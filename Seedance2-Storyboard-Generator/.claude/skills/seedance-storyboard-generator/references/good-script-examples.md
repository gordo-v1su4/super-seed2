## I. Core Hook
Desperate counterattack, classic wuxia, revenge satisfaction drama.

## II. Story Synopsis
**Setting:** Late Northern Song dynasty, Cangzhou prison camp, a brutal winter blizzard.
**Opening conflict:** Lin Chong, former commander of the Imperial Guard, is framed and exiled to Cangzhou to guard a hay depot—unaware assassins are closing in.
**Protagonist portrait:** Lin Chong, a master martial artist who endures everything, only wants to serve his sentence and return home, pushed to the edge of collapse.
**Main plot:** Blizzard collapses hay depot shelter → refuge in mountain temple → overhears enemy conspiracy → patience breaks, deadly counterattack.
**Ending:** Slays his enemies, abandons hope of returning to court, joins the outlaws on a snowy night.

## III. One-Line Selling Point
Enough is enough—no more patience! On a night of wind and snow, Leopard Head Lin Chong draws his spear, blood splashes across the mountain temple!

## IV. Character Profiles

### Lin Chong
**Visual look:** Mid-thirties, weathered resolute face, worn cotton coat, felt hat, long spear in hand, wine gourd at waist.
**Background:** Former commander of the Imperial Guard, now a Cangzhou prisoner.
**Core tags:** Suppressed rage, tragic hero, killing god unleashed.
**Personality:** Once submissive and law-abiding; when cornered, erupts with explosive resolve.
**Signature line:** "This world forces good men to become demons!"

### Lu Qian
**Visual look:** Sharp narrow face, rich brocade robes, cloak.
**Background:** Trusted agent of Marshal Gao, Lin Chong's former "friend."
**Core tags:** Betrays friends for profit, sinister schemer.
**Role:** Escalates conflict, serves as the sacrifice of revenge.

## V. Script Outline
**Act I (Setup):** Lin Chong guards the hay depot alone; harsh environment, lonely oppressive mood.
**Act II (Rise/Turn):** Snow collapses his hut; he takes refuge in the mountain temple and accidentally overhears Lu Qian's plot to burn the depot.
**Act III (Climax):** Hearing the truth, rage consumes him; he bursts from the temple and slays Lu Qian and his men in the blizzard.
**Act IV (Resolution):** Lin Chong walks away with spear in hand, vanishing into endless snow—the outlaw path begins.

## VI. Full Script

### Episode 1
**1-1 DAY EXT. Hay Depot**
Props: long spear, wine gourd
Characters: Lin Chong

△ 【Establishing shot】Ink-wash style Cangzhou outskirts, snow falls like salt, world reduced to black and white.
△ Extreme wide overhead, a few broken huts alone on the snowfield, hay stacked like hills around them.
△ Camera pushes in; Lin Chong wears a felt hat and worn straw cloak, leaning against a hay pile alone.
△ He lifts his head, eyes deep with exhaustion and helplessness; breath vapor vanishes instantly.
Lin Chong (OS): Serve my sentence, survive this winter, return to the capital…
△ A gust hits; snow slaps his face; he pulls the cloak tighter.
△ He shakes the wine gourd at his waist—empty.
△ Wind howls louder, like a beast's growl; depot flags snap in the gale.
【Subtitle: Cangzhou Hay Depot — Guard Lin Chong】
△ Lin Chong takes his spear and walks into the deepening snow, lonely silhouette.

### Episode 2
**2-1 DAY EXT. Hay Depot Ruins**
Props: long spear, wine gourd
Characters: Lin Chong

△ Continues from prior episode; Lin Chong returns from town with wine, boots crunching snow.
△ He carries a full gourd; a rare warmth on his face.
△ Suddenly he stops; pupils contract sharply.
△ Ahead, both huts collapsed under snow into rubble, broken beams scattered.
Lin Chong (startled): This…
△ He rushes forward, digs through ruins, face goes pale.
Lin Chong (to himself): If I hadn't gone for wine, I'd be buried here now?
△ Wind howls; embers in the rubble flicker.
△ He stands, looks around—endless snow, nowhere to shelter.
△ His gaze lifts to the hillside; a broken old temple barely visible.
Lin Chong: I'll take refuge at the mountain temple for one night.
△ He grips his spear, turns uphill, steps heavy and urgent.

### Episode 3
**3-1 NIGHT INT. Mountain Temple**
Props: fire starter, wine gourd, large stones
Characters: Lin Chong

△ 【Subtitle: Mountain Temple】
△ Old temple door creaks open.
△ Lin Chong enters; dim light reveals broken deity statue, cobwebs everywhere.
△ He wedges large stones against the door until sealed tight, exhales slightly.
△ He builds a fire before the statue; flames crackle on dry branches.
△ He uncorks the gourd, drinks deep; firelight on his stern face.
△ Outside, wind wails like ghosts.
△ Spear across his knees, eyes closed, fighting cold.
△ Suddenly footsteps on snow outside—soft, but piercing in the silent night.
△ Eyes snap open; right hand grips the spear shaft; gaze turns sharp.
△ Outside, shadows move at the door crack, thrown on broken walls like demons.

### Episode 4
**4-1 NIGHT INT/EXT. Mountain Temple**
Props: long spear, torch
Characters: Lin Chong, Lu Qian (VO), Fu An (VO), jail warden (VO)

△ Lin Chong holds breath, rises silently, presses to the door crack to listen.
△ Voices carry through wind and snow, clear.
Lu Qian (VO): Once this fire is lit, Lin Chong will have no grave!
Jail warden (VO): Rest easy, Commander Lu—even if fire doesn't kill him, losing the depot is a death sentence!
Fu An (VO): Marshal Gao wants his head to take back!
△ At "Marshal Gao," Lin Chong trembles; patience in his eyes becomes raging fire.
△ 【Flashback】Marshal Gao's son harassing his wife, wrongful arrest, exile to Cangzhou—images rush past.
【Flashback end】
△ Knuckles white on the spear shaft; teeth grind audibly.
Lin Chong (OS): Lu Qian! I treated you well—and you want me dead!
△ Outside, oil splashes; firelight reddens the door crack.
△ Lin Chong looks up, killing intent sky-high; despair gone.
△ No hesitation—spear in hand, kicks the door stones aside.

### Episode 5
**5-1 NIGHT EXT. Outside Mountain Temple**
Props: long spear, severed head
Characters: Lin Chong, Lu Qian, Fu An, jail warden

△ Door bursts open, wood splinters fly.
△ Lu Qian's trio were setting the fire; the crash terrifies them.
△ Lin Chong charges like a wrathful god; spear flashes cold.
Lin Chong (roaring): Traitors! Die!
△ Lu Qian staggers back; Lin Chong spears the jail warden through the chest; blood sprays white snow.
△ Fu An tries to flee; Lin Chong spins—spear butt or blade—cuts his legs; he wails in snow.
△ Lu Qian kneels, face twisted in terror.
Lu Qian (trembling): Brother Lin! It was the Marshal's order—not me!
△ Lin Chong expressionless, eyes ice-cold; spear drives through Lu Qian's throat without pause.
△ Blood stains snow—a stark contrast to ink-wash background.
△ 【Establishing shot】Wind and snow still rage, burying bodies and blood.
△ Camera pulls back; Lin Chong walks away with spear and gourd, never looking back.
【Subtitle: Driven to the Marshes】
△ Freeze on Lin Chong's resolute back—vast sky and earth, one lonely trail of footprints.
