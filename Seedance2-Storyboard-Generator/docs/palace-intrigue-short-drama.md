## Part 1: Template + Theory

Great short dramas are never random inspiration — they are carefully designed emotional detonations.

> 1. \[Genre type\],\[Style type\];
>
> 2. Picture (0–5s): \[Shot type\] + \[Character action\] + \[Expression/eyes\] + \[Environment detail\]
>
> 3. Dialogue 1 (\[Character A\], \[Emotion tag\]): \[Line\]
>
> 4. Inner OS (\[Character\]): \[Unspoken inner thought\]
>
> 5. Picture (6–10s): \[Shot type\] + \[Character action\] + \[Expression/eyes\] + \[Environment detail\]
>
> 6. Dialogue 2 (\[Character B\], \[Emotion tag\]): \[Line\]
>
> 7. Inner OS (\[Character\]): \[Unspoken inner thought\]
>
> 8. Picture (11–15s): \[Shot type\] + \[Character action\] + \[Freeze frame\]
>
> 9. Sound: \[BGM style\] + \[Key SFX\] + \[Voice character\] + \[Ambient sound\]
>
> 10. Duration: Exactly 15 seconds (\[Emotional tone\], \[Rhythm description\])

Many people see this wall of fields and freeze — but this is not a pile of text boxes. It is a director's workstation built for AI.

A few more notes on the template:

**First, always add a style prefix.** I used to spend 100 words describing palace-intrigue mood; now I write: `Period palace intrigue, live-action short drama;`

Style prefix format: **genre type + style type**

Genre types (pick one):

> Urban angst romance · Urban domineering CEO · Urban sweet romance · Urban revenge · Urban underdog comeback · Period angst romance · Period palace intrigue · Period fantasy · Period wuxia · Military tearjerker · Campus youth · Tearjerker family · Yandere obsession · Time-travel rebirth · Suspense brain-burn

Style types (common picks):

> Live-action short drama · Period short drama · Fantasy short drama · Suspense short drama

Combine one of each, for example:

> `Urban angst romance, live-action short drama;` `Period palace intrigue, live-action short drama;` `Time-travel rebirth, live-action short drama;` `Tearjerker family, live-action short drama;`

**Second, use contrasting inner OS.** Give characters a split between speech and thought — the bigger the gap, the brighter the spark.

Plain version: Empress says, "Sister, you are so thoughtful." OS: "She really is thoughtful."

Contrast version: Empress says, "Sister, you are so thoughtful." (gentle) OS: "This cup of poison — sister, drink it slowly." (cold smile)

**Third, control emotional rhythm** or the video tastes like plain water.

Theory done — on to the demo.
