# Yan Qing Fights on the Arena — AI Short Drama Script

## Story Summary

The rogue Yan Qing seeks revenge for a friend and travels to Tai'an Prefecture to challenge Ren Yuan, the undefeated arena champion of two years, winning through skill rather than brute force.

---

## Character Profiles

| Character | Look | Personality |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Yan Qing | 25, balanced build, handsome features, fine brocade attire | Clever, agile, highly skilled, loyal |
| Ren Yuan | 35, eight chi tall, bulging muscles, bald | Arrogant, immensely strong, domineering |
| Waiter | 20, thin and quick | Timid but loves gossip |
| Crowd | Mixed types | Happy to watch the spectacle |

---

## Scene 1: Tai'an Prefecture Inn

**【Setting: Inn main hall, daytime】**

**Shot 1: Wide**
The inn buzzes; guests sit in clusters, debating loudly.

**Shot 2: Medium**
The waiter wipes tables while animatedly telling guests a story.

**Waiter**
(excited)
Honored guests, guess what? Ren Yuan put out word again! Whoever knocks him off the platform gets a thousand taels of gold on the spot!

**Shot 3: Close**
Yan Qing sits in a corner with a tea bowl, lost in thought.

**Shot 4: Extreme close**
Yan Qing sets down the bowl; resolve flashes in his eyes.

**Yan Qing**
(quietly, to himself)
Ren Yuan — two years is enough. Time to collect.

---

## Scene 2: Below the Arena

**【Setting: Tai'an arena, daytime, huge crowd】**

**Shot 1: Extreme wide**
The platform rises high; spectators pack every side; banners flap; drums and gongs thunder.

**Shot 2: Medium**
Ren Yuan stands center stage, shirtless, muscles like iron.

**Ren Yuan**
(smirking)
Two years! Tai'an, Shandong, Hebei — who doesn't know my Iron Arm technique? Who still dares come up and die?

**Shot 3: Close**
The stands fall silent; no one answers.

**Ren Yuan**
(cold laugh)
What? Nobody? Then I declare—

**Shot 4: Medium**
A light figure vaults onto the platform.

**Yan Qing**
(lands, steady)
Hold on!

**Shot 5: Close**
The crowd gasps; Ren Yuan narrows his eyes.

**Ren Yuan**
Where did this green kid crawl from? Name yourself!

**Yan Qing**
(cupped hands)
Yan Qing of Liangshan — here to learn from you!

---

## Scene 3: Arena Duel

**【Setting: On the platform, tense and fierce】**

**Shot 1: Medium**
Ren Yuan closes in; size difference is crushing.

**Ren Yuan**
Yan Qing? I've heard you're known — but before me Ren Yuan, you're an ant!

**Shot 2: Close**
Yan Qing unbowed, footwork light.

**Yan Qing**
Ant or not — we'll see after we fight. Brother Ren — please!

**Shot 3: Wide**
Ren Yuan roars and charges, arms like hammers.

**Shot 4: Slow motion**
Yan Qing sidesteps; movement flows like water.

**Shot 5: Medium**
Ren Yuan's punch misses; Yan Qing is already behind him.

**Ren Yuan**
(spins, roaring)
Stop playing tricks!

**Shot 6: Close**
Yan Qing's corner of mouth lifts; eyes relaxed.

**Yan Qing**
All's fair in war, Brother Ren.

---

## Scene 4: Fight Escalates

**【Setting: On the platform】**

**Shot 1: Rapid cuts**
- Ren Yuan hammers blow after blow
- Yan Qing dodges left and right, body like wind
- Spectators stare, stunned

**Shot 2: Medium**
Ren Yuan pants, sweat on his brow.

**Ren Yuan**
Stand still! Stand still and fight me!

**Shot 3: Close**
Yan Qing untouched, at ease.

**Yan Qing**
Brother Ren — you're too slow.

**Shot 4: Wide**
Ren Yuan, enraged, unleashes his finisher — Mount Tai crushes the head, leaping high.

**Ren Yuan**
Die!

**Shot 5: Low angle**
Yan Qing does not retreat — he advances into Ren Yuan's descent.

---

## Scene 5: One Move Decides It

**【Setting: On the platform】**

**Shot 1: Close (slow motion)**
Ren Yuan falls from the sky, momentum terrifying.

**Shot 2: Close (normal speed)**
Yan Qing taps off the ground, body twists, both hands catch Ren Yuan's waist.

**Shot 3: Medium**
Borrow force — Yan Qing spins; Ren Yuan loses balance.

**Shot 4: Close**
Terror in Ren Yuan's eyes.

**Ren Yuan**
This can't be—!

**Shot 5: Wide**
Yan Qing exhales sharply and hurls Ren Yuan off the platform!

**Yan Qing**
Off you go!

**Shot 6: Slow motion**
Ren Yuan arcs through the air and crashes to the ground.

---

## Scene 6: Victory

**【Setting: On the platform】**

**Shot 1: Close**
Silence — then thunderous cheers.

**Shot 2: Medium**
Yan Qing stands center, cupped hands to the crowd.

**Yan Qing**
I was fortunate!

**Shot 3: Close-medium**
The referee announces the result.

**Referee**
Winner — Yan Qing the Rogue!

**Shot 4: Close**
Yan Qing gazes into the distance; faint sadness in his eyes.

**Yan Qing**
(inner monologue)
Brother — your debt is paid.

**Shot 5: Wide**
Yan Qing leaps off the platform and vanishes into the crowd.

---

## Ending

**【Black screen, subtitles】**

*"Win by skill, persuade by virtue"*
*"Yan Qing the Rogue — hero of Liangshan"*

**【End】**

---

## Script Highlights

1. **Tight pacing:** Six scenes, 2–3 minutes each, ~15 minutes total
2. **Modern dialogue:** Colloquial, suited to today's audience
3. **Clear visuals:** Action beats defined for AI generation
4. **Emotional hook:** Brotherhood, weak beats strong
5. **Thematic lift:** Not just fighting — justice versus tyranny
