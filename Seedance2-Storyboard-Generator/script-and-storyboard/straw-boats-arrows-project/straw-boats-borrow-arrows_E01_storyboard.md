**0-3s: Confrontation in the handsome tent - setting up the scene**
The camera enters the commander's tent in Soochow, and the military order is clearly visible on the commander's case. Zhuge Liang walked in slowly with feather fan and silk scarf, his expression calm and indifferent. Light enters through the gaps in the tent, creating dramatic light and shadow.

**3-6s: Making things difficult and setting up a trap - the plot unfolds**

**6-9s: Military Order - Conflict Escalation**
Zhou Yu's eyes were bright and he was secretly happy, pretending to be surprised on his face. Zhuge Liang approached Shuai An and picked up his brush. Close-up of the brush strokes on the military order, the handwriting is vigorous and powerful. Zhou Yu had a sneer on his lips and picked up the wine glass, with murderous intent in his eyes.

**9-12s: Drinking Fight - Emotional Climax**
Two glasses collide and the wine splashes in slow motion. A close-up of Zhou Yu's eyes with murderous intent, compared to Zhuge Liang's smiling face. Split screen comparison: Zhou Yu is proud vs Zhuge Liang is calm. Detailed close-up of the swaying feather fan.

**12-15s: Closing - Suspense ending**
