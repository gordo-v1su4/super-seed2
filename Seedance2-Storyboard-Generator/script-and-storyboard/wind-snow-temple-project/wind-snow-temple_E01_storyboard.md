## Seedance Prompt

2D Chinese animation style, similar to "The Great Protector" and "Five Elements in the Mist Mountain", cel shading, clear lines, bright but calm colors, flat color matching, 15 seconds, 9:16 vertical screen, lonely and desolate atmosphere

0-3 seconds: scene establishment - distant view, Cangzhou hayyard is covered with snow (@image_file_3), endless white sky and earth, haystacks are covered with snow, several thatched huts stand alone, the camera slowly moves forward, and heavy snow falls, creating a lonely and desolate atmosphere

3-6 seconds: The protagonist appears - Lin Chong (@image_file_1) walks out of the thatched hut, wearing a felt hat and a sheepskin jacket, holding a flower gun (@image_file_5). The flower gun is slanted behind him, with a wine gourd hanging on his waist (@image_file_6). The camera follows from behind and then switches to the front to mid-shot to show his resolute and solemn face.

6-9 seconds: Daily labor - Lin Chong uses a flower gun to push away the snow on the hay pile, and carefully checks whether the hay is damp. The camera follows his movements and inserts a close-up of his hands wearing gloves. The wind blows his cloak and snowflakes accumulate on his shoulders.

9-12 seconds: Emotional moment - Close-up of Lin Chong's face (@image_file_2), staring at the vast snowy scene, his eyes full of loneliness and depression. He took off the wine gourd and took a sip. The camera slowly zoomed in, and background music played softly.

12-15 seconds: Ending - distant view, Lin Chong stands alone in the vast snow (@image_file_3 rear view), holding a gun on his back, his small figure facing the endless white world, the screen displays the subtitle "Forage Wind and Snow", reflecting loneliness and endurance

[Sound] A sad Chinese bamboo flute solo, accompanied by weak string accompaniment + the sound of howling wind, trampling on the snow, and the tapping of flower guns + dialogue: "Be patient for a little longer...waiting for the expiration of the period..."

## Last frame description

**Subject:** Lin Chong's back figure standing alone in the vast snow, with his spear slanted on his back, and his sheepskin coat covered with snow

**background:** Endless white snow, piles of grass covered with snow, several thatched houses in the distance

**Light:** Cool-toned natural light, shimmer reflected from the snow

**composition:** Vertical perspective composition, the characters occupy a small part of the screen, highlighting the vastness of the world and the insignificance of individuals

**atmosphere:** Loneliness, desolation, and forbearance
