# Wind, Snow, and the Mountain Temple (Desperate Counterattack) - Script

## I. Core Hook
Jedi counterattack, unbearable

## II. Story Synopsis

**Story Background:** During the Northern Song Dynasty, Lin Chong, the instructor of the 800,000 Imperial Army, was framed by Gao Qiu and assassinated in Cangzhou. While guarding the forage depot in Cangzhou, Gao Qiu still refused to let him go and sent Lu Qian and others to set fire to him and kill him.

**Opening conflict:** Lin Chong guards the Cangzhou forage depot in heavy snowfall alone. Heavy snow collapsed his residence, and he was forced to take shelter in the mountain spirit temple.

**Portrait of the Protagonist:** Lin Chong, nicknamed "Leopard Head", was the former instructor of the 800,000 Imperial Army, with strong martial arts skills and a calm and tolerant nature. After being framed and assassinated, he still had the hope of returning to the court and tolerated and retreated at every turn.

**Main event:** The heavy snow collapsed thatched hut → Hidden in the mountain spirit temple → Encountered an enemy who came to set fire → Overheard the conspiracy Dialogue → The retreat was cut off → Killed the enemy in anger → Throwing himself into the Liangshan Mountains in the snow.

**Ending:** Lin Chong killed his enemy with his own hands, completely cut off any illusions about the imperial court, and fled to Liangshan on a snowy night, completing the complete transformation from "enduring humiliation" to "resistance".

## III. One-Line Pitch
On a snowy night, forage depot, I can endure None and finally explode! The leopard head angrily kills the enemy, and the desperate counterattack becomes a legend!

## IV. Character Profiles

### Lin Chong
**Visual Image:** In his thirties, eight feet tall, leopard head and ring eyes, swallow jaw and tiger whiskers, commanding presence. Wearing a bamboo hat, a parrot green shirt, a sheepskin jacket, a luan belt around his waist, and leopard skin boots.

**Identity Background:** The former 800,000-strong Forbidden Army instructor was framed for assassination in Cangzhou because Gao Qiu's adopted son Gao Yanei coveted his wife.

**Core Tags:** endure humiliation, high martial arts, honest and loyal, endure and give in

**Personality:** He is calm and tolerant by nature, giving in everywhere, but still has the desire to return to the court. He has superb martial arts skills and superb marksmanship. When cornered, his explosive power is astonishing.

**Signature Line:** "God's way is good! We will find out today!"

### Lu Qian
**Visual Image:** He is in his thirties, with a sinister face and sinister eyes. He is wearing a dark official uniform, a cape, and a short sword at his waist.

**Identity Background:** Gao Qiu's confidant, Lin Chong's former "friend", is actually a sinister villain.

**Core Tags:** Insidious, treacherous, fake and powerful

**Personality:** On the surface, he and Lin Chong are brothers, but in fact, he was framed repeatedly by Gao Qiu's orders. Cruel and ruthless, he will do whatever it takes.

### Fu An
**Visual Image:** He is in his twenties, with a pointed mouth and monkey cheeks, shifty rat-like eyes, wearing a uniform of a police officer, and holding a torch.

**Identity Background:** Cangzhou police officer, Lu Qian’s accomplice.

**Core Tags:** Follow the trend and help others to do evil.

### jail overseer
**Visual Image:** He is in his forties, sarcastic, wearing uniforms, with a flattering smile on his face.

**Identity Background:** Cangzhou prison camp jail overseer.

**Core Tags:** Greedy, snobbish, bullying and shying away from the strong

## V. Script Outline

**Episode 1 (Setup):** Lin Chong is guarding the Cangzhou forage depot in heavy snowfall and harsh environment, but he still has hope of returning to the court.

**Episode 2 (Rising Action):** Heavy snow collapsed Lin Chong's residence, and he hid in the mountain spirit temple for temporary shelter. At this time, his enemy Lu Qian and others arrived and prepared to set fire to him.

**Episode 3 (Turn):** Lin Chong overheard the Dialogue of Lu Qian and others outside the temple in the temple, and learned that Gao Qiu wanted to kill him, and all escape routes were cut off.

**Episode 4 (Climax):** Lin Chong burst into anger, rushed out of the temple, and killed Lu Qian, Fu An and other enemies with his own hands, completely releasing years of suppression.

**Episode 5 (Resolution):** After Lin Chong killed his enemy, he fled to Liangshan on a snowy night, completing the transformation from "endurance" to "resistance".

## 9. Visual style suggestions

**Overall style:** 2D Chinese animation style, similar to "The Great Protector" and "Five Elements in the Mist Mountain", cel shading, clear lines, bright but calm colors, flat color matching, highlighting the desolation of the wind and snow and the hero's loneliness.

**Key scene visuals:**
- Snowy scene: celluloid-style snowflake effect, clear outlines
- Fighting scenes: fast editing, sharp shooting skills, and dynamic lines to express the sense of speed
- Emotional scene: Close-up of Lin Chong’s eyes changing from forbearance to outburst
