## Seedance Prompt

2D Chinese animation style, similar to "The Great Protector" and "Five Elements in the Mist Mountain", cel shading, clear lines, bright but calm colors, flat color matching, 15 seconds, 9:16 vertical screen, crisis and distress atmosphere

0-3 seconds: Night falls - long shot, night scene of the forage field (@image_file_3), the wind and snow intensify, the wind howls, the snow falls harder, the camera shakes slightly to simulate the wind, Lin Chong is preparing to sleep in his clothes in the thatched cottage, the warm candlelight contrasts with the cold outside

3-6 seconds: The thatched house collapses - there is a sudden roar, the roof of the thatched house is collapsed by heavy snow (@image_file_3), Lin Chong (@image_file_1) desperately rolls to escape, debris is flying everywhere, dynamic movement of the camera, scenes of chaos and destruction

6-9 seconds: Moving forward with difficulty - Lin Chong is walking through a blizzard, carrying a flower gun (@image_file_1) and a wine gourd on his shoulders. The wind and snow are beating against him. The camera follows his struggle at a low angle. The snowflakes accumulate quickly, reflecting isolation and danger.

9-12 seconds: Arriving at the ancient temple - the mountain temple (@image_file_4) appears in the distance, a dilapidated building, Lin Chong approaches tiredly, the camera follows from behind, and then shows the temple entrance, broken walls, and snow-covered roofs

12-15 seconds: Temporary shelter from the wind and snow - In the temple (@image_file_5), Lin Chong blocked the door with a stone (@image_file_7), sat down against the statue, lit a small bonfire, the warm firelight swayed on his tired face, the screen displayed the subtitle "Snow-laden Thatched Cottage", a brief moment of peace

[Sound] Tense orchestral music with drums and bass strings + the sound of roof collapse, howling wind, footsteps on snow, friction of stones, crackling of fire + dialogue: "Not good!" "I almost died under the snow today..."

## Last frame description

**Subject:** Lin Chong leaned against the mountain god statue, with a small bonfire burning beside him, looking tired.

**background:** Inside the dim mountain temple, there are mottled mountain god statues and broken walls.

**Light:** The warm light of the bonfire contrasts with the cold moonlight outside the temple

**composition:** Vertical mid-shot composition, with characters occupying the middle of the screen and statues of gods as the background frame

**atmosphere:** Temporary peace, but still lurking crisis
