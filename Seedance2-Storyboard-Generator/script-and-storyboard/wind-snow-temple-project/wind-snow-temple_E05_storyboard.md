## Seedance Prompt

2D Chinese animation style, similar to "The Great Protector" and "Five Elements in the Mist Mountain", cel shading, clear lines, bright but calm colors, flat color matching, 15 seconds, 9:16 vertical screen, firm and reborn atmosphere

0-3 seconds: Aftermath - Three corpses are covered in snow, with blood stains visible on the snow (@image_file_6 rear view), Lin Chong (@image_file_1) stands among them, chest heaving violently, the camera slowly sweeps across the scene, the wind and snow have softened, and it feels like the battle is over

3-6 seconds: Reminiscence - Close-up of Lin Chong's hands (@image_file_3), covered in blood, then took out the rag left by his wife (@image_file_6), rubbed it gently, his eyes were full of sadness but soon replaced by determination, showing his inner monologue through his expressions

6-9 seconds: Awakening - medium shot of Lin Chong, looking at the burning hayfield in the distance, then looking back at the temple, realizing - there is no retreat, no longer patient, with a determined expression on his face (@image_file_3), the morning light begins to appear

9-12 seconds: Departure - Lin Chong arranges his felt hat, picks up the flower gun (@image_file_1), takes one last look at the mountain temple, and then turns around resolutely, the camera follows his departure, the snowflakes begin to settle, and the road ahead is clear

12-15 seconds: New life begins - distant view, Lin Chong walks into the vast snowy scene (@image_file_2, @image_file_4), with his spear slanted on his back, and his figure gradually disappears into the distance. The screen displays the subtitle "Up Liangshan on a Snowy Night" with the subtitle "From now on, Liangshan has a leopard head", it feels like the beginning of a new chapter

[Sound] Victorious but melancholy Chinese orchestral music + the sound of snowflakes falling, footsteps fading away, the gentle sound of wind, the first bird song at dawn in the distance + inner monologue: "In this world, there is no place for me, Lin Chong!" "Liangshanshuibo is my new life, Lin Chong!"

## Last frame description

**Subject:** Lin Chong's back gradually disappeared on the mountain road in the wind and snow, his steps were firm

**background:** The vast snowy land, the looming mountain peaks in the distance, and the faint morning light on the horizon

**Light:** There is a hint of warm morning light in the cool colors, symbolizing rebirth

**composition:** Vertical perspective composition, the characters are very small and blend into the vast world

**atmosphere:** Determination, rebirth, and the birth of a hero
