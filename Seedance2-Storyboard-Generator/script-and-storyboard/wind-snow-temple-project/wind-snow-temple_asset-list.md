## 6. Style consistencyDescription

1. **Color unity:** All materials adopt the main colors of black, white and gray of traditional Chinese ink painting, with appropriate use of color embellishments (such as the red of the gun tassel, the orange-red of the flame)

2. **Lighting treatment:** Dramatic light and shadow contrast, clear light and dark, highlighting key points

3. **Texture rendering:** The texture of snow, leather and metal must be clearly identifiable

4. **Atmosphere creation:** The desolation of the wind and snow, the loneliness of the hero, and the intense feeling of the explosion should be throughout.

5. **Character recognition:** Lin Chong’s green jersey, bamboo hat, and spear are identification signs; Lu Qian’s sinister expression and dark official uniform are identification signs.
