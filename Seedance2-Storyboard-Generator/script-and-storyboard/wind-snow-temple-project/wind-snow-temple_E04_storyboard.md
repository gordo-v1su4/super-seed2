## Seedance Prompt

2D Chinese animation style, similar to "The Great Protector" and "Five Elements in the Mist Mountain", cel shading, clear lines, bright but calm colors with red embellishments, flat color matching, 15 seconds, 9:16 vertical screen, intense cathartic atmosphere

0-3 seconds: Breaking out - the temple door is suddenly pushed open, Lin Chong (@image_file_4) strides out, with a flower gun (@image_file_8) in hand, the three villains are panicking, the camera dynamic moves closer to Lin Chong's intimidating presence, and snowflakes are flying around him

3-6 seconds: First kill - Fu'an rushes up with a knife, Lin Chong's flower spear stabs out like lightning (@image_file_3), the tip of the spear pierces the throat, slow-motion impact, blood splashes on the white snow, the camera follows the movement of the flower spear, fast and deadly

6-9 seconds: Kill again - Cha Di turns around to run away, Lin Chong kicks him down (@image_file_3), the tip of the gun is pressed against the throat, Lin Chong's cold eyes are close-up (@image_file_2), with a quick stab, the second villain falls, the rhythm and momentum accumulate layer by layer.

9-12 seconds: Confrontation - Lu Qian kneels in the snow (@image_file_4), begging for his life, Lin Chong stands condescendingly, with a gun pointed directly at his heart, dramatic low-angle shot, the flames of the burning forage field are visible in the distance (@image_file_7), contrast between red and white

12-15 seconds: Execution - Close-up of Lin Chong's face (@image_file_2), the anger that has been suppressed for many years explodes, the spear is stabbed hard, Lu Qian's screams suddenly stop, Lin Chong looks up to the sky and screams, the screen displays the subtitle "Kill the enemy with anger", the ultimate catharsis.

[Sound] Epic orchestral battle music, with heavy drums and brass + the sound of temple doors being broken, the stabbing of flowers and guns, the sound of blood splattering, screams, trampling on the snow, the crackling of fires in the background + dialogue: "You...how are you still alive?!" "Gao Qiu caused my wife to scatter and destroy the family!" "Happy! Happy!"

## Last frame description

**Subject:** Lin Chong stood in the snow, with three corpses lying at his feet, looking up to the sky and screaming.

**background:** The light of the burning hayyard illuminates the snow, and the mountain temple looms in the distance.

**Light:** The orange-red of the fire contrasts sharply with the cold white of the snow

**composition:** Vertical screen elevation composition, the character is in a heroic posture, with firelight behind him

**atmosphere:** Catharsis, relief, complete transformation from tolerance to resistance
