## Seedance Prompt

2D Chinese animation style, similar to "The Great Protector" and "Five Elements in the Mist Mountain", cel shading, clear lines, bright but calm colors, flat color matching, 15 seconds, 9:16 vertical screen, shock and awakening atmosphere

0-3 seconds: Temporary rest - Lin Chong (@image_file_1) took a nap against the statue of the god (@image_file_3) in the temple, with his eyes closed for a short rest. In the dark room, the flickering firelight gradually became fainter, creating a quiet but ominous atmosphere.

3-6 seconds: Unexpected break-in - footsteps and voices are heard outside the door, Lin Chong's eyes suddenly open (@image_file_1 close-up), alert and nervous, the camera slowly moves closer to his face, the sound comes through the crack in the door, a sense of danger

6-9 seconds: The truth is revealed - through the crack in the door, there is a shadow moving outside the door (@image_file_4), the voice becomes clear, Lu Qian's voice is discussing the conspiracy of burning the fodder yard, Lin Chong's expression changes from confusion to shock to understanding, and the inner struggle is clearly visible

9-12 seconds: The pain of betrayal - a close-up of Lin Chong's face (@image_file_2), with suppressed anger burning in his eyes. When he heard that his wife was still being held captive, his years of patience suddenly collapsed. The extinguished firelight cast a dramatic light and shadow, and the emotions accumulated layer by layer.

12-15 seconds: Determination is made - Lin Chong stood up slowly, held the flower gun tightly, walked towards the door, and removed the stone on the top of the door (@image_file_5). The camera followed his firm movements, and the screen displayed the subtitle "Picture Poor Dagger Seeing", the transition from patience to resistance.

[Sound] Gradually tense bass drum and discordant strings + footsteps outside the door, vague human voices, crackling of fire, friction of stones, sound of clenching gun barrels + dialogue (fuzzy): "When a fire burns down the forage yard..." "Lieutenant Gao wants him to die..."

[Reference] @image_file_1 facial expression reference, @image_file_2 angry expression reference, @image_file_3 inside the temple reference, @image_file_4 outside the temple reference, @image_file_5 props reference

**background:** In the dim temple, a faint snow light shines through the door

**Ambience:** Awakening, anger, volcano about to erupt
