# Wu Song Fights the Tiger - Script
## I. Core Hook
Desperate counterattack

## II. Story Synopsis

**Story Background:** During the Northern Song Dynasty, tigers injured many people in Jingyang Ridge, Yanggu County, Shandong Province. The government posted an official notice for hunting tigers, but none dared to respond.

**Opening Conflict:** Wu Song passed by the innkeeper at the foot of Jingyang Ridge and drank eighteen bowls of "Three Bowls and You Cannot Cross the Ridge" liquor. The innkeeper persuaded him to stay overnight, saying that there were tigers on the mountain that would hurt people. Wu Song was so drunk that he didn't believe there was a tiger, so he insisted on going up the mountain.

**Portrait of the Protagonist:** Wu Song, a native of Qinghe County, is eight feet tall and has a dignified appearance. The second person in the ranking is called "Wu Erlang". He has a bold and strong personality, an amazing capacity for drinking, and strong martial arts skills. When drunk, he becomes even more wild and domineering.

**Main event:** Wu Song was drunk and lying on the bluestone → the tiger suddenly pounced → Wu Song fought the tiger with his bare hands → broke the staff → beat the tiger to death with his bare hands → became a tiger fighting hero.

**Ending:** Wu Song dragged the dead tiger down the mountain and was carried down the mountain by the hunters. From then on, he became famous as a tiger hunting hero.

## III. One-Line Pitch
After eighteen bowls of strong wine, I encountered a tiger on Jingyang Ridge! Fighting with bare hands, killing with iron fist becomes a legend!

## IV. Character Profiles

### Wu Song
**Visual Image:** Twenty-five or six years old, eight feet tall, with a face like a crown of jade, eyes like stars, thick eyebrows and big eyes. Wearing a green gauze turban, a parrot-green war robe, a pair of turtle-back silver belts around his waist, and a pair of powder-soled deerskin boots.

**Identity Background:** Born in Qinghe County, he fled to other places for beating people to death. He was later amnesty and passed by Jingyang Ridge on his way back home.

**Core Tags:** Bold and strong, amazing drinker, strong martial arts, daring

**Personality:** He has an upright personality, hates evil very much, has an amazing capacity for drinking, and becomes even more wild and uninhibited after being drunk. Do not believe in evil, do not fear authority, and speak with strength.

**Signature Line:** "Even if there is a tiger, I'm not afraid!"

### tiger
**Visual Image:** Hanging eyes and white forehead A big tiger, one foot long, with yellow and black fur and bright stripes. Its eyes are like copper bells, its fangs are exposed, and its muscles are knotted. It is extremely ferocious.

**Identity Background:** The Jingyang Ridge tiger has injured countless people and has become a serious problem in the local area.

**Core Tags:** Fierce and cruel, powerful and agile

**Personality:** Hungry, ferocious, and extremely aggressive, specializing in picking out lone people.

### innkeeper
**Visual Image:** About fifty years old, bald and slightly fat, wearing an apron, with a sad face and worried eyes.

**Identity Background:** The owner of the innkeeper at Jingyang Ridge brews "Three Bowls and You Cannot Cross the Ridge" spirits.

**Core Tags:** Kind, honest, and sincere

**Personality:** He is kind-hearted, worried about the safety of his guests, and his kind words are often misunderstood as wanting to keep more guests.

## V. Script Outline

**Act I (Setup):** Wu Song went into the inn to drink, and drank eighteen bowls in a row. The innkeeper dissuaded him from staying overnight, but Wu Song insisted on going up the mountain.

**Act II (Rising Action):** Wu Song went up the mountain drunkenly, and saw the official notice confirming the presence of a tiger, but refused to look back because of his face, and lay drunk on the bluestone.

**Act III (Climax)(Climax):** The tiger suddenly pounced, Wu Song woke up to dodge, the staff broke off the tree, fought with the tiger with bare hands, rode on the tiger and struck hard, and finally killed the tiger.

**Resolution:** Wu Song was exhausted and dragged the dead tiger down the mountain. The Orion was amazed when he found it.
