## Seedance Prompt

3D animated style similar to Kung Fu Panda, vibrant colors with dramatic sunset to moonlight transition, 15 seconds, 9:16 aspect ratio, ominous and tense atmosphere

0-3s: scene establishment - Wide shot of mountain path at sunset (@image_file_3), golden orange light, long shadows, forest silhouettes against colorful sky, Wu Song (@image_file_2) walking unsteadily up the path, camera high angle showing isolated figure, wind blowing leaves

3-6s: Discovery - Medium shot as Wu Song approaches mountain shrine (@image_file_4), weathered wooden structure with official proclamation (@image_file_6) posted, camera pushes in toward the text, Wu Song's face leaning in to read, expression shifting from confusion drunk to shock

6-9s: Realization and conflict - Close-up of Wu Song's face (@image_file_2), sweat appearing on forehead, eyes widening with fear, internal conflict visible, camera quick cuts between his face and the tiger warning text, dramatic side lighting emphasizing his dilemma

9-12s: Fateful decision - Wu Song pauses, looks back down the path, then forward up the mountain, close-up of his face hardening with stubborn pride, teeth gritted, camera dramatic upward angle as he makes choice to continue

12-15s: Rest - Wide shot of large flat rock (@image_file_7) under moonlight, Wu Song lying down, staff dropped beside him, fast asleep, forest surrounding with mysterious shadows, stars appearing in dark sky, serene but dangerous atmosphere, text overlay "Drunk Lying on Blue Stone"

[Sound] Ominous wind music with subtle tension + Sound effects of wind howling, leaves rustling, crickets beginning + Internal monologue: "It turns out there really is a tiger..." "What are you afraid of!"
