## Seedance Prompt

3D animated style similar to Kung Fu Panda, vibrant colors, dramatic lighting, 15 seconds, 9:16 aspect ratio, heroic and energetic atmosphere

0-3s: scene establishment - Wide shot of Jingyang Ridge inn exterior, red lanterns fluttering in wind, "Three Bowls of Dogs" signboard clearly visible, sunny day with blue sky, camera slowly pushes in towards the entrance, welcoming and lively atmosphere

3-6s: Character introduction - Wu Song (@image_file_1) strides into the inn, tall and muscular, wearing green battle robe, confident heroic stance, camera tracks his movement from behind, then cuts to medium shot as he sits at table and places wooden staff with a loud thud, powerful presence

6-9s: Action sequence - Montage of Wu Song drinking, innkeeper (@image_file_2) pouring wine from jar (@image_file_1), Wu Song lifting bowl (@image_file_2) and drinking in one gulp, slamming bowl down, repeatedly, camera quick cuts between pouring, drinking, emptying bowls, building excitement and rhythm, warm lantern light of interior (@image_file_4)

9-12s: Climax moment - Close-up of Wu Song's flushed face, fierce heroic expression, 18 empty bowls visible on table, innkeeper counting with shocked expression, Wu Song stands up swaying slightly but still powerful, camera dramatic upward angle showing his dominance

12-15s: Conclusion - Wu Song grabs his staff and walks toward door, back view, innkeeper watching with concern, warm interior light transitioning to bright exterior, sense of impending adventure, text overlay "Three bowls are not enough"

[Sound] Epic heroic Chinese orchestral music with drums and flute + Sound effects of bowl slamming, liquid pouring, footsteps + dialogue: "Store, bring the wine!" "Good wine!"

## Last frame description

**Subject:** Wu Song walks out of the restaurant with a whistle stick in his back, walking boldly

**background:** The wooden door frame of the restaurant, the sun is shining brightly outside, and the mountain road winds upwards

**Light:** Transition from warm lantern light indoors to bright natural light outdoors

**composition:** The vertical screen is centered in the composition, the character occupies the lower half of the screen, and the door frame serves as the frame

**atmosphere:** A sense of boldness and anticipation of adventure.
