## Seedance Prompt

3D animation style, similar to "Kung Fu Panda", epic climax of hero's victory, dramatic moonlight lighting, 15 seconds, 9:16 frame ratio, victorious but tired atmosphere

0-3s: Turning point - The tiger (@image_file_4) pounces with killing intent, Wu Song (@image_file_1) dodges to the side at the perfect time, grabs the back of the tiger's neck, uses the momentum to flip and knock the beast to the ground, the camera follows the smooth movement for continuous shots, powerful and dynamic

3-6s: Struggle - A close-up of the tiger's huge claws waving wildly, claws scratching the ground, dust and stones flying, Wu Song (@image_file_1) struggled to maintain control, his muscles bulged with exertion, sweat dripped, his face showed both effort and determination, the camera captured the fierce struggle with a dramatic angle

6-9s: Climax - a rapid succession of punches: Wu Song's iron fist comes down again and again, each impact is accompanied by the tiger's roar of pain, blood spurting from the mouth and nose, the camera switches between Wu Song's angry face and the tiger's weakened struggle, rapid rhythmic editing increases the intensity, Wu Song shouts "Death! Die! Die!"

9-12s: Victory - The tiger's body twitched and then stopped, there was silence, Wu Song (@image_file_2) fell next to the dead tiger (@image_file_5), his chest heaved, his clothes were torn and bloody, every feature was full of exhaustion, the moonlight illuminated the warrior and the defeated beast, the camera slowly zoomed out to show the consequences

12-15s: Ending - Wu Song (@image_file_3) slowly stood up, grabbed the tiger carcass, and dragged it down the mountain road. The long shadow stretched under the full moon (@image_file_7), and the silhouette of the hero under the night sky. The camera followed from behind, and the figure disappeared into the distance. The text was superimposed with "Hero who defeated the tiger", and the final subtitle: "Wu Song defeated the tiger and spread it all over the world."
