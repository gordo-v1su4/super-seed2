1. Core Hook
Desperate counterattack, classic martial arts, and revenge drama.

2. Story Synopsis
Story background: In the late Northern Song Dynasty, Cangzhou Prison Camp, a cold winter with wind and snow.
Opening conflict: Lin Chong, the leader of the 800,000-strong Forbidden Army, was framed and sent to Cangzhou, where he was ordered to guard the forage farm, but he did not know that the murderous intention was coming.
Portrait of the Protagonist: Lin Chong, who is highly skilled in martial arts but tolerates nothing. He just wants to serve his sentence and return home. Faced with the ensuing persecution, he is on the verge of collapse.
Main event: Heavy snow in the forage yard collapsed the residence -> Temporarily took refuge in the mountain temple -> Unexpectedly heard the enemy's conspiracy -> The tolerance value was cleared and the Jedi counterattacked.
Ending: Kill the enemy with your own hands, completely cut off the illusion of returning to the court, and go up to Liangshan on a snowy night.

3. One-Line Pitch
You can tolerate None, but you must endure None! On a snowy night, leopard-headed Lin Chong unsheathed his spear, splattering blood on the mountain temple!

4. Character Profiles
Lin Chong
Visual image: He is in his thirties, with a resolute face and a vicissitudes of life. He is wearing a shabby cotton-padded jacket and a felt hat on his head, holding a flower gun and a wine gourd hanging from his waist.
Identity background: Former 800,000-strong instructor of the Forbidden Army, now a prisoner in Cangzhou.
Core tags: Tolerance, Tragic Hero, The Coming of the God of Death.
Characteristics: Originally willing to compromise and keep things to himself, after being forced into a desperate situation, he showed great explosiveness and determination.
Golden sentence: "The way of this world forces good people to become ghosts!"
Lu Qian
Visual image: Pointed mouth, monkey cheeks, wearing rich brocade clothes and a cloak.
Identity background: Gao Taiwei's confidant, Lin Chong's former "friend".
Core tags: Selling friends for glory, sinister villain.
Function: Intensify conflicts and serve as revenge sacrifice.

5. Script Outline
Act I (Setup) (Setup): Lin Chong guards the forage farm alone. The environment is harsh and he feels lonely and depressed.
Act II (Rising) (Rising Action/Turning Point): The thatched hut collapsed under the weight of snow, and Lin Chong was forced to go to the mountain temple for temporary refuge. He accidentally broke up the plot of Lu Qian and others to burn the hayyard.
Act III (Climax) (Climax): When Lin Chong heard the truth, he was so angry that he rushed out of the temple and killed Lu Qian and others in the snow.
Epilogue (Ending): Lin Chong raised his gun and walked away, disappearing into the wind and snow. From then on, the world was far away.
