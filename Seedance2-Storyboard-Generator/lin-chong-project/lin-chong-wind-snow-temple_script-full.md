# Lin Jiaotou Fengxueshan Temple - Script (Full Version)

## 1. Core Hook
Desperate counterattack

## 2. Story Synopsis

**Story background**: Cangzhou during the Xuanhe period of the Northern Song Dynasty. Lin Chong, the leader of the 800,000-strong Forbidden Army, was framed by Captain Gao Qiu and sent to Cangzhou to guard the fodder farm.

**Opening Conflict**: On a snowy winter night, the forage yard was collapsed by heavy snow, and Lin Chong temporarily stayed at a nearby mountain temple to escape the cold.

**Portrait of the Protagonist**: Lin Chong, the former instructor of the 800,000-strong Forbidden Army, with a leopard head and ring eyes, a swallow jaw and a tiger beard. He was highly skilled in martial arts but tolerated no concessions. He only looked forward to returning to his hometown to reunite with his wife after serving his sentence.

**Main plot event**: Lin Chong was taking shelter from the cold in the mountain temple → He overheard Lu Qian and others plotting to burn himself to death outside the door → Recalling the step-by-step pressure of Setup Gao Qiu and his son → He could bear none and burst out in revenge → Killing his enemies with his own hands → He was forced to go to Liangshan.

**Ending**: Lin Chong took the heads of his enemies and defected to Liangshan. He completely bid farewell to Coach Lin who forbeared and retreated and became the hero of Liangshan.

## 5. Script Outline

**Act I (Setup)**: On a snowy night, the forage field guarded by Lin Chong collapsed due to heavy snow. None went to the mountain temple to escape the cold.

**Act II (Rising Action)**: Lin Chong was lighting a fire to keep warm in the temple, and accidentally heard Lu Qian and others outside the door plotting to burn him to death.

**Act III (Climax) (Turning Point/Climax)**: Lin Chong recalled the various persecutions of Gao Qiu and his son in Setup. His forbearance completely collapsed. He rushed out of the temple with a gun and killed his enemies with his own hands.

**Epilogue (End)**: Lin Chong surrendered to Liangshan with the heads of his enemies, bid farewell to forbearance, and embarked on the road to Liangshan.
