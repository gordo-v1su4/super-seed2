# Lin Jiaotou Fengxueshan Temple - Script

## Production parameters

| Parameter | Value |
|------|-----|
| Visual style | Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading |
| Aspect ratio | 9:16 vertical screen |
| Emotional tone | Suspense and tension |
| Duration of each episode | 15 seconds |
| Total number of episodes | 5 episodes |

---

## Script structure

### Episode 1: Forage Farm in the Snow

**Emotional tone:** Loneliness and depression

**Duration:** 15 seconds

**Key plot:**
- The snowy Cangzhou forage farm
- Lin Chong was guarding alone, looking lonely.
- It’s windy and snowy, and the environment is bleak

**First frame:** Aerial shot from a distance, a panoramic view of the snowy forage field

**Last frame:** Lin Chong is wearing a raincoat and holding a spear, standing alone in the wind and snow, his back lonely

---

### Episode 2: Return from selling wine

**Emotional tone:** Surprise and uncertainty

**Duration:** 15 seconds

**Key plot:**
- Lin Chong returned from buying wine in the market
- Found the hayyard collapsed by heavy snow
- After being shocked, I decided to go to the mountain temple to take refuge temporarily.

**First frame:** Medium shot of Lin Chong walking from the snow (continued from the last frame of the previous episode)

**Last frame:** Lin Chong stood in front of the collapsed hayyard, with a solemn expression, and turned towards the mountain temple in the distance.

---

### Episode 3: Temporary refuge in the temple

**Emotional Tone:** The calm before the storm

**Duration:** 15 seconds

**Key plot:**
- Lin Chong enters the dilapidated mountain temple
- Light a fire to keep warm and drink wine to ward off the cold
- The wind howls outside the temple, hiding murderous intent

**First frame:** Lin Chong pushes open the dilapidated wooden door of the mountain temple (continued from the last frame of the previous episode)

**Last frame:** Lin Chong is sitting by the fire in the temple, holding a wine gourd, and the figures outside the temple gate are shaking.

---

### Episode 4: Conspiracy outside the temple

**Emotional Tone:** Shocked and Angry

**Duration:** 15 seconds

**Key plot:**
- Lin Chong heard someone talking outside the door
- Identified the voices of Lu Qian and others
- Learned about Gao Qiu’s plot to burn himself to death

**First frame:** Lin Chong is in the temple, his ears pricked up and his expression alert (continued from the last frame of the previous episode)

**Last frame:** Lin Chong's eyes widened with anger, holding a spear in his hand, his whole body trembling, his eyes burning with anger

---

### Episode 5: Snowy Night Revenge

**Emotional tone:** A heroic explosion

**Duration:** 15 seconds

**Key plot:**
- Lin Chong rushed out of the temple gate
- Start a fierce battle with Lu Qian and others
- Kill the enemy, walk away in the snow, and walk up Liangshan

**First frame:** Lin Chong suddenly pushed open the temple door, with a spear in his hand and full of murderous intent (continued from the last frame of the previous episode)

**Last frame:** Bodies are scattered on the snow, Lin Chong’s back disappears into the wind and snow, and the world is vast.

---

## Emotional arc overview

```
Loneliness and depression → Surprise and uncertainty → Calm before the storm → Shock and anger → Heroic outburst
    ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓
  E1 E2 E3 E4 E5
```

## Color planning

| Number of episodes | Main color | atmosphere |
|------|--------|------|
| E1 | Gray white, jet black | Cold and lonely |
| E2 | Gray white, light blue | Suspicious |
| E3 | Warm yellow (fire), gray and blue exterior | Hidden crisis |
| E4 | Dark red (anger), jet black | Anger accumulation |
| E5 | Bright red (blood), pure white | Explosion and fate |
