This material list is based on the following historical records:
1. "History of the Song Dynasty" Biography of Lu Xiufu
2. "The Chronicles of Song Dynasty"
3. "Ya Shan Zhi"
4. Relevant archaeological discoveries and academic research

**Key historical information**:
- Time: The sixth day of February in the second year of Xiangxing in the Southern Song Dynasty (1279)
- Location: Yashan (now Yamen Town, Xinhui District, Jiangmen City, Guangdong Province)
- Main characters: Lu Xiufu (44 years old), Zhang Shijie (50s), Zhao Bing (7 years old)
- Historical significance: Marking the end of the Southern Song Dynasty, one of the most tragic dynasty changes in Chinese history
