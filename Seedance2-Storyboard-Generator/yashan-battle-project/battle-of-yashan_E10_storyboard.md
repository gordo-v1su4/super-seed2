## Series Summary

**Story theme**: National integrity that would rather die than surrender, a tragic historical farewell

**Historical Significance**:
- The Battle of Yashan marked the end of the Southern Song Dynasty
- One hundred thousand soldiers and civilians threw themselves into the sea and died for their country, showing the spirit of preferring to live in pieces rather than in ruins.
- One of the most tragic dynastic changes in Chinese history

**Educational Value**:
- Loyalty and integrity
- Dignified choice in the face of desperate situations
- The heaviness and reflection of history
