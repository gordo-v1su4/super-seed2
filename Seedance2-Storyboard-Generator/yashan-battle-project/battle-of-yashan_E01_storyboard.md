## Seedance Prompt

```
Epic Chinese traditional ink wash painting style with heavy historical atmosphere and tragic tone, similar to "Night Revels of Han Xizai" color system and "A Thousand Li of Rivers and Mountains" composition, Song Dynasty period, cinematic grandeur. The tragic story of Yashan Sea Battle in 1279.

0-3s: Historical Prologue - Emerging slowly from the darkness @image_file_1 The magnificent scene on the cliff mountain and the sea. Under the cloudy sky, thousands of Song Army warships were arranged in a huge array, stretching across the sea like the Great Wall on the sea. The camera slowly advances to show the final military power of the Southern Song Dynasty.

3-6s: Demonstration of momentum - The camera continues to advance, and the details of the warship gradually become clear: the dragon decoration on the bow, the fluttering battle flag, and the soldiers on the deck ready to go. The waves rolled and the sea breeze roared, creating a depressing atmosphere before the war.

6-9s: Time and space positioning - text appears on the screen: "Early February of the second year of Xiangxing in the Southern Song Dynasty (1279), Yashan". The camera zooms up to look down, showing the whole picture of the Song Army Navy, with thousands of sails competing, majestic but tragic.

9-12s: Astronomical Warning - The clouds in the sky are getting lower and lower, and on the horizon in the distance, the dark shadows of Yuan army warships are faintly visible, like dark clouds pressing over the city. The camera pans from the Song Army array to the distant sea level, suggesting an approaching crisis.

12-15s: Ending hook - the camera freezes in the center of the Song Army array, on the most gorgeous royal boat (dragon boat), the golden dragon pattern is particularly eye-catching under the clouds, paving the way for the introduction of the young emperor in the next episode.

Sound design:
-Music: A low and sad Guqin solo, combined with the faint sound of waves and wind in the distance
- Sound effects: the sound of waves lapping against the side of the ship, the sound of flags blown by the wind, and the faint sound of thunder in the distance
- Narrator (optional): "In the second year of Xiangxing, the last resistance force of the Southern Song Dynasty gathered in Yashan..."
```

## Last frame description

**scene**: On the sea surface of Yashan, there is a panoramic view of the Song Army's naval array. In the center of the picture is a gorgeous royal boat, and on the horizon in the distance can be seen the faint shadow of the Yuan Army's warships.

**Screen**:
- Main subject: Yuzhou (dragon boat) in the center of the picture, decorated with golden dragon patterns
- background: An array of thousands of Song warships, a cloudy sky
- Long shot: The dark shadow of the Yuan army on the horizon
- Light: Cloudy, dim and depressing light
- composition: The royal boat is in the center, and the warship array is symmetrically distributed
- atmosphere: depressing, tragic, stormy and imminent

**Purpose**: As the opening of E02, introduce Young Emperor and Lu Xiufu.
