# Sima Guang smashes the vat - script

## Story Overview

During the Northern Song Dynasty, the young Sima Guang was playing with his friends in the courtyard. The little friend accidentally fell into a large water tank and was in critical condition. All the children panicked, but Sima Guang remained calm and wisely broke the water tank with a stone and successfully rescued his friend who fell into the water.

## Style positioning

**Visual style**: Traditional Chinese ink animation style, blended with the bright colors and smooth movements of modern animation, similar to the aesthetic style of "Havoc in Heaven".

**Target duration**: 60-90 seconds, divided into 4-6 episodes

---

## Four-act structure (starting, transitioning, and ending)

### Act 1: Starting - Opening Introduction (0-15 seconds)

**scene Setting**: The courtyard of an official’s house during the Northern Song Dynasty

**Content**:
- On a sunny spring afternoon, the ancient trees in the courtyard tower into the sky
- A group of children wearing Song Dynasty costumes are playing in the courtyard
- 7-year-old Sima Guang (intelligent and calm) chases and plays with other friends
- There is a huge water tank placed in the corner of the courtyard, the water surface is as calm as a mirror

**atmosphere**: Cheerful, peaceful, and childlike

---

### Act 2: Cheng - Accident happens (15-30 seconds)

**Content**:
- Children's games become more and more intense
- A child (about 5-6 years old) approached the water tank while chasing
- The child climbed onto the edge of the tank to look down and accidentally lost his balance.
- With a "plop", the child fell into the water
- There is a huge splash on the water surface, and the water splashes in all directions

**Drama Conflict**: Sudden accident breaks the calm

---

### Act 3: Turn - Crisis (30-50 seconds)

**Content**:
- The child who fell into the water struggled in the water and his cry for help was weak.
- Other children panicked, some cried, some ran away
- Someone shouted: "Call the adults!"
- Time is running out, and the child who fell into the water gradually sank into the water.
- Sima Guang remained calm and looked around for solutions.
- He found a big stone on the ground

**Climax**: A critical moment of life and death

---

### Act 4: Together - Saving people with wit (50-75 seconds)

**Content**:
- Sima Guang lifted the big stone and smashed it into the water tank with all his strength
- There was a loud "crash" sound and the water tank broke.
- Water gushes out of the tank, and the child who fell into the water rushes out with the water.
- All the children gathered around and helped the rescued child up.
- The rescued child coughed up water and regained consciousness
- All the children looked at Sima Guang with admiration
- Sima Guang wiped the sweat from his forehead and showed a happy smile

**Theme Sublimation**: Be resourceful and brave, and stay calm in times of crisis.

## Core information

**Theme**: Stay calm in times of crisis, be resourceful and brave

**Values**:
- Stay calm in times of trouble
- Good at thinking about solutions
- Brave and decisive action

**Cultural background**: Sima Guang (1019-1086), a politician and historian in the Northern Song Dynasty, presided over the compilation of "Zizhi Tongjian". "Sima Guang smashed the vat" was a widely circulated allusion in his childhood.
