## Seedance Prompt

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, traditional Chinese martial arts aesthetic with atmospheric mountain landscape and emotional farewell, 15 seconds, 16:9 aspect ratio, atmosphere of bittersweet departure and new beginnings

0-3s: [scene Creation] Wutai Mountain is shrouded in clouds and mist. The gate of Wenshu Monastery stands quietly on the green mountainside. The morning mist covers the mountains like a veil. The red temple walls are looming in the mist. Several distant bells are heard from the temple and echo for a long time in the valley, bringing with it the detachment and transcendence of the Buddhist pure land.

3-6s: [Subject introduction] Medium shot, Lu Zhishen is standing outside the mountain gate. He is more than half drunk, carrying a simple luggage package on his back, and holding the Zen staff that has been with him for many years. He slowly turns back to look at Elder Zhizhen. His burly body is actually a bit rickety at this moment. Deep guilt and apology flash in his eyes. He lowers his head and whispers, "Elder... Sajia, Sajia has realized his mistake."

6-9s: [Deepening of Emotions] Elder Zhizhen stepped forward and gently placed a sealed letter in Lu Zhishen's rough hands. His kind eyes showed understanding and expectation, and he slowly said, "Zhishen, you are not a Buddhist. Please bring this letter to Zen Master Zhiqing at Daxiangguo Temple in Tokyo. Go, there may be your blessing there." A close-up of Lu Zhishen took the letter, his eyes slightly red.

9-12s: [Climax scene] Long shot, deep shot, Lu Zhishen bows deeply to say goodbye to Elder Zhizhen, then turns around resolutely and steps onto the stone path leading to the bottom of the mountain. A medium shot follows his back, and after walking a few steps, he looks back at Wenshu Monastery, the monastery that took him in and restrained him. His inner monologue is heard off-screen, "Mount Wutai, the Sa family will come back!", with reluctance and pride in his voice.

12-15s: [Freezing frame at the end] Long shot of fixed shot, Lu Zhishen's back gradually disappears deep into the mist-shrouded mountain road, and finally merges into the vast scenery of Mount Wutai, leaving only the long bells echoing in the valley, and the subtitles slowly appear "The world is far away, see you later". The story of a hero ends here, and a new journey has begun.
