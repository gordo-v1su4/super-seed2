## Seedance Prompt

Chinese ink wash painting style mixed with anime cel-shading, traditional Chinese martial arts aesthetic with bold brushstrokes and dramatic lighting, 15 seconds, 16:9 aspect ratio, atmosphere of drunken revelry and impending chaos

0-3s: [scene Creation] Long shot fixed shot, the majestic Wutai Mountain stretches across the screen, the green mountains are stacked on top of each other, the red walls and yellow tiles of Wenshu Monastery can be seen vaguely standing on the mountainside among the clouds and mist, the sun shines golden beams through the clouds, and the majestic landscape artistic conception

3-6s: [Subject introduction] The camera quickly zooms in to the tavern at the foot of the mountain. Lu Zhishen is sitting on a bench with his chest exposed and one foot on the bench. His burly body almost occupies half of the frame. He holds up a porcelain bowl and drinks. The wine overflows from his beard and flows down his chin, wetting the front of the gray monk's robe. He is full of boldness and boldness.

6-9s: [Plot development] Close-up shot, Lu Zhishen slams the empty bowl on the table, and the "bang" sound makes the peanuts on the table jump. He staggers to stand up due to the surge of alcohol, his eyes are blurred but showing the excitement of drunkenness, his rosy face is full of drunkenness, and his face is stained with alcohol on his whiskers and beard.

9-12s: [Emotional progression] Long shot, Lu Zhishen staggers onto the mountain road, his body is crooked but his steps are heroic, humming a tuneless Jianghu ditty, his figure disappears and appears in the green mountains and forests, and the Wenshu Monastery in the distance stands quietly, foreshadowing the coming storm.

12-15s: [Freeze at the end] Lu Zhishen’s drunken back gradually faded away, and finally disappeared at the corner of the mountain road, leaving only the majestic figure of Mount Wutai and a vague sigh. The prelude to the trouble caused by alcohol has begun

[Sound] The melodious guqin at the beginning → turns into cheerful folk music → and ends with a hint of ominous bass, the sound of bowls falling, footsteps, and the sound of mountain and forest wind
