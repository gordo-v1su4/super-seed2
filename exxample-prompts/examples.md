# Best successful prompts for dance

Real, high-performing examples adapted from X, Reddit, and test guides. They lean on refs and timelines for stronger results.

**How to use:** Upload references first, then copy a block below and swap placeholders (`@Image1`, `@Audio1`, `@Video1`, etc.) for whatever tags your pipeline uses (e.g. Seedance `@image_file_1`, `@audio_file_1`, `@video_file_1`). Many patterns come from viral cinematic/dance threads and `awesome-seedance`-style repos.

---

## Neon street dance solo (classic viral style — image + audio)

```text
A street dancer based on the look of @Image1, wearing a black hoodie, on a rainy night street lit by neon. The background shows a blurred crowd, and the wet ground reflects the lights. 0–3s: Subtle warm-up movement, shoulders following the beat. 3–7s: The beat from @Audio1 drops – footwork and jumps. 7–10s: Rhythm intensifies, fast spin and landing. 10–15s: On the beat drop, a final freeze. The camera mirrors the music: handheld tracking at the start → whip pan on accents → slow push for the closing. Color particles burst on the beat hits. Maintain character consistency, perfect music sync, realistic physics, and cinematic lighting with a strong sense of rhythm.
```

*Solo hip-hop/street: character tends to stick; rain/clothes physics read well in tests.*

## Group dance battle / choreo transfer (video ref for moves + image refs)

```text
Two dancers based on the looks of @Image1 and @Image2 perform an energetic street dance battle with exact choreography and movements transferred from @Video1, perfectly synced to the rhythm and beats of @Audio1. 0–4s: They circle each other with subtle footwork and shoulder pops. 4–8s: Build-up with synchronized spins and jumps. 8–12s: Intense face-off with fast pops and freezes on every beat drop. 12–15s: Final group freeze pose. Cinematic night street setting with neon lights and wet reflections. Camera: dynamic orbiting tracking shot that follows the energy, whip pans on accents. Maintain perfect character consistency, realistic fabric movement and gravity, no deformities, strong rhythmic lighting.
```

*Multi-ref battle pattern: video ref carries complex motion; good for choreo transfer.*

## Latin / cinematic couple dance (timed + audio sync)

```text
A passionate Latin dance couple based on the look of @Image1 (man) and @Image2 (woman), executing smooth spins, dips, and lifts exactly synced to @Audio1. 0–5s: Slow intimate build-up with close holds and basic steps. 5–10s: Rhythm intensifies – fast turns and dramatic lifts. 10–15s: Climax with synchronized spins and final elegant pose. Elegant ballroom with soft dramatic lighting and subtle fog. Camera: smooth tracking shot circling the couple, slow push-in on emotional beats. Hollywood cinematic style, realistic physics on flowing dress and body weight, perfect music sync, maintain proportions and consistency.
```

*Partner dances: camera + fabric/weight read strongly when timed to audio.*

## Anime-style dance with motion ref (experimental)

Anime-style girl dancer based on the look of @Image1 performing vibrant K-pop choreography transferred from @Video1, movements perfectly timed to @Audio1 beats. White studio cyclorama with strobe lights and glossy floor. 0–3s: Energetic intro poses. 3–8s: Full choreo with sharp pops and hair flips. 8–12s: High-energy drops and spins. 12–15s: Final powerful freeze. *Prompt-library style: refs help lock look without as much style drift.*
