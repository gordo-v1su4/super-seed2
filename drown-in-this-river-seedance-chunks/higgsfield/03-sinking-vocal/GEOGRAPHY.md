# Chunk 03 — where is this? (geography)

One location, not a hard cut from forest to something else.

## The problem to solve

Gens often read **too narrow and too shallow** — like a creek in a slot canyon. That breaks the story: she must fall into **deep** floodwater with room for a full **underwater** sequence (chunk 04+), not a splash in a puddle.

**Fix:** The log crosses a **small rocky notch on the rim** — but below is a **wide, deep drowned city basin**. Narrow at the bridge; **vast and deep** under the fall.

---

## The place (side view)

```
  [ storm forest on BLUFF RIM ]     giant root tree on rock spur
              |                              |
         rocky NOTCH  ← storm tree fell here
              |
    ==========LOG (bridge span)=============   ← she walks OUT over the drop
              |
              |   ~80–120 ft sheer drop
              |
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    ~  DEEP FLOOD BASIN — drowned city  ~
    ~  submerged buildings, dark channel  ~
    ~  river 30ft+ deep in main stem      ~
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
              → river flows through ruined streets
```

- **Wide basin below** — not a skinny gorge floor. Think dam burst / urban floodplain: rooftops, facades, debris in **deep** churning water.
- **Log is high** — trunk runs from cliff lip toward the far root tree, **over the deepest part of the main channel**.
- **Fall** — backward off the log into **open deep water**, not a shallow pool at the bottom of a crack.

---

## Why is she on the log?

| Beat | Story logic |
|------|-------------|
| **Rim walk** | She has been on a **bluff trail** in storm woods — gorge/basin hidden by trees. |
| **Clearing** | Trees thin at the **notch**. First sense of height; city lights flicker far below. |
| **Why step on** | Rim path **ends at the notch**. A mega-tree fell in the storm — trunk is the **only crossing** along the rim toward the root tree spur. City lights pull her forward. |
| **Why walk out** | She needs to cross OR get a clear view of what happened below. Each step takes her **farther over the deep channel** — she does not yet see how high until the finale reveal. |
| **Reveal** | Zoom/orbit: the “narrow” crossing was only at the rim — below is the **whole drowned city** in **deep** flood. |
| **Break** | Lightning hits rim tree/trunk. Wood fails. She falls **backward into the deep basin** — setup for chunk 04 impact and later underwater. |

**Not:** random twig over a creek. **Not:** mudslide. **Preferred break:** tree/trunk snap (original beat).

---

## Depth lock (for Image 2 / regen)

When the water reads wrong, push these reads:

- **Submerged full building heights** — windows, roofs, facades under surface
- **Dark teal-black water** — you cannot see bottom; depth implied
- **Main channel** through streets — current, debris trains, whitecaps
- **High-angle fall** (chunk 04): tiny figure vs **vast** flood surface, not a forest pond

Cross-section panel (environment sheet row 1) should show **cliff → long drop → deep basin**, not cliff → shallow puddle.
