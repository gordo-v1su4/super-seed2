```
  [ storm forest on BLUFF RIM ]
              |
         rocky NOTCH — trail ends
              |
    ~~ undercut lip (flood eroded base) ~~
         leaning storm tree at edge
              |
    ---- lip SLIDES — mud/rock slurry ----
              |
              |   ~80 ft drop
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    ~  WIDE DEEP FLOOD BASIN — drowned city  ~
    ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
         flood undercut caused the slide
```

- **Wide deep basin below** — room for fall + underwater.
- **Rim failure** — localized **undercut slide** at the lip (water undermined the base), not a megalog bridge walk.
- **Tree** — already at the compromised edge; **tips with the slide**; she **grabs it** — brief **hang** — lightning — **sheer** — fall.

## Why does she end up on the tree?

| Beat | Story logic |
|------|-------------|
| **Rim walk** | Bluff trail — basin hidden. |
| **Clearing** | Notch overlook — city lights below. |
| **At edge** | Hand on storm tree at rim — ground feels wrong (undercut). |
| **Slide (~10s)** | Lip **gives way** — mud/rock slurry — tree **tips** — she **grabs trunk** as footing goes. |
| **Hang (~11s)** | **One beat** clinging — tree only support over abyss. |
| **Final phrase** | Lightning flash on wet wood → root **sheers** → fall backward into deep basin. |

**Not:** walking a canyon bridge. **Not:** trunk explosion. **Not:** long Tarzan hang.
