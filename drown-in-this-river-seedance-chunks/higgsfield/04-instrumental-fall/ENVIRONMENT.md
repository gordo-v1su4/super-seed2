# Chunk 04 — environment lock (Image 2 / fall shots)

Reference for mood grid and regen notes when water reads wrong.

## Wrong (last gen issue)

- Calm **turquoise / blue** water
- Still forest pond or peaceful lake under the fall
- No debris, no urban wreckage
- Looks like a dive into a swimming hole

## Right

- **Massive flood river** — fast, violent, storm-fed
- A **city destroyed by the flood** — submerged rooftops, broken walls, drowned streets
- **Debris dragged in the current** — timber, metal, building fragments, wreckage trains
- Dark **teal-black / muddy churn**, whitecaps and foam — crushed grade, not pretty blue
- High-angle fall shots: she drops toward **urban flood devastation**, not nature water

## Good reference panels (operator gens)

Use silent Image 2 mood grid with panels like:

1. Wide — woman falling toward **flooded city**, lightning optional in grid only
2. Wide — **submerged ruins**, debris in torrent, storm rain
3. Underwater — sinking past **building silhouettes** and wreckage in murky teal (chunk 05+ overlap)
