## In Seedance prompts

- `@Image 2` = geography **and** color grade lock

## Wrong reads

- Crushed near-black (`#040D12`, `#000000`) — panels unreadable
- Flat blue-gray monochrome
- Calm turquoise pool / swimming-hole blue
- High-frequency speckle / grain soup in shadows and water
- Grade that brightens or cools randomly on finale beats

Environment sheet = **empty plates** at these hex values. Yellow dress = **@Image 1** in video gens; **yellow swatch in legend only** on the sheet (no figure).
